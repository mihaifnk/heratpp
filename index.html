Privacy Policy for Emergency Room Queue App

Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to safeguard your data.

Information Collection and Use

The Emergency Room Queue App collects information to provide better services to all our users. The types of personal information we collect include:

  • Location data to verify updates provided by users are within the vicinity of a hospital.
  • Device information for troubleshooting and improving app performance.

We use the information we collect primarily to provide, maintain, protect, and improve our current services, and to develop new ones.

Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of our app except in the following cases:

  • With user consent.
  • For legal reasons, like complying with law enforcement.

Your Privacy Rights

Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal information. Our app provides settings to manage your privacy controls.

Security

We are dedicated to protecting the security of your information and take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
