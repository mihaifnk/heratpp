Privacy Policy for Emergency Room Queue App

Introduction

Our Emergency Room Queue App is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the use of your data, emphasizing our commitment to not collecting or storing any personal information.

Location Data

The only data our app accesses is your device's location. This is used solely to verify that updates provided by users are within the vicinity of a hospital. Importantly, we do not store or keep any record of your location data.

No Data Collection or Storage

Aside from temporary access to location data, our app does not collect, store, or process any personal information. We believe in minimal data usage and ensure that your privacy is respected at all times.

Security

We take the security of our app seriously. Even though we do not collect personal data, we implement strong security measures to prevent unauthorized access and ensure the integrity of our location verification process.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and encourage you to review our policy periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us.
